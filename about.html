About My Art

My artwork follows my daydreams. With each piece, individuals are transported into a new world, each one different from the last. The concept of daydreaming has always been something I hold close to my heart. The freedom of being able to explore imagination and escape into another world fascinates me. When reality feels like too much, my work lets anyone escape. Throughout my pieces, I try to bring fantastical worlds to your fingertips. My artwork is mostly digital art with some elements of traditional mediums. I mainly work in coding languages like JavaScript and HTML, the Adobe Suite, and video. For traditional art I work with printmaking, costume design, drawing, and painting.
